Travel Tips For Vietnam

Planning a trip to Vietnam? Here are some essential tips to help you make the most of your journey through this beautiful country.

Before You Go

Visa Requirements

Most visitors need a visa to enter Vietnam. You can apply for an e-visa online or get a visa on arrival. Make sure to check the latest requirements based on your nationality and the length of your stay.

Pro Tip: Apply for your visa at least 2 weeks before your trip to avoid any last-minute issues.

Best Time to Visit

Vietnam's climate varies by region. The best time to visit is generally from November to April when the weather is cooler and drier. However, each region has its own ideal visiting season.

Pro Tip: Pack for all weather conditions as you might experience different climates while traveling from north to south.

Getting Around

Transportation

Vietnam has various transportation options including domestic flights, trains, buses, and motorbikes. For longer distances, consider taking overnight trains or buses to save time and accommodation costs.

Pro Tip: Download the Grab app for reliable taxi and motorbike taxi services in major cities.

Cultural Etiquette

Local Customs

Vietnamese culture places importance on respect and politeness. Learn a few basic Vietnamese phrases, dress modestly when visiting temples, and always ask permission before taking photos of locals.

Pro Tip: Remove your shoes before entering someone's home or a temple, and use both hands when giving or receiving items.

Health & Safety

Staying Healthy

Drink bottled water, be cautious with street food, and consider getting travel insurance. Make sure your routine vaccinations are up to date and consider additional vaccinations like Hepatitis A and Typhoid.

Pro Tip: Carry hand sanitizer and wet wipes for situations where soap and water aren't available.
